Composting Basics

Compost is a nutrient rich, dirt-like material that enriches soil and nourishes growth.

Organic materials like food scraps and leaves decompose naturally over time; while this process takes a considerable amount of time in nature, we can generate the ideal composting environment very easily at home! Learn more about the ins and outs of composting at Common Ground Compost.
